Purpose statement

The purpose of the website is to provide information to visitors about the climatic conditions, keep a historical record of these conditions and provide predictions for the coming days for three regions in southeastern Idaho. The website is also oriented to be a valuable tool to know the meteorological phenomena that can cause alert.
