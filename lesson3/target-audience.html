Target Audience

Our target audience is:

  • People who live in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, or who are intent to travel to these areas.
  • Both genders.
  • Aged 14 years or older.
  • Students or with at least full secondary school complete
  • Any family status.
  • Any level of income.
